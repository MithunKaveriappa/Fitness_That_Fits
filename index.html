Fitness Recommendation Prototype

As soon as the workouts of the fitness enthusiast people is done, they don’t know what kind of food they have to consume to make their workout worthy. The kids due to the busy schedule of their parents they are not getting essential nutritious food for long time sustainability and also the adults who are having blood pressure, diabetes, thyroid etc they are restricted by foods and they don’t know what they need to consume to maintain all the health level stable.We present this application, a preliminary platform for Healthy Food recommendation, which benefits from the dataset and its rich variety of categorized food labels, thereby enabling fitness Healthy Food recommendations predicated on the users’ preferences and their recent viewing behavior. The proposed model also incorporates an approach to produce diversified recommendations and foster the practice of distinct fitness activities based on like-minded users, based on BMI, similar users search, random recommendation, search recommendation’ information. An initial experimental study shows the trade-offs of the hybrid approach considered.
